About I-Track

I-Track is an innovative platform designed to streamline the monitoring of intern teachers. With real-time tracking, attendance management, and supervisor oversight, it ensures an efficient and transparent internship experience.

Key Features

Real-Time Tracking

Keep supervisors and interns connected through live status updates.

Attendance Management

Track time-in/time-out records for reliable monitoring.

Supervisor Oversight

Enable supervisors to view and manage their assigned interns effectively.
